Frequently Asked Questions
What is the best way to learn vocabulary?
Consistent practice with flashcards, reading, and usage helps improve vocabulary effectively.
How often should I review words?
Review words regularly, ideally daily, to reinforce memory and retention.
Can I learn vocabulary without a teacher?
Yes! Self-study with flashcards, apps, and reading materials is very effective.
